My Top 5 Favorite Movies

My favorite classics that defined cinema, romance, and thrills.

The Ranked List

  1. Love & Basketball

    Genre: Romance / Drama / Sports

    A romantic drama following Monica and Quincy, two basketball players who juggle chasing their professional dreams while trying to keep their hearts intact.

  2. Dirty Dancing

    Genre: Romance / Drama

    The perfect summer romance flick featuring undeniable chemistry, dancing, and a timeless soundtrack. Nobody puts Baby in a corner!

  3. The Sixth Sense

    Genre: Thriller / Horror

    A legendary supernatural thriller by M. Night Shyamalan, about a young boy who can see dead people. Easily one of the most iconic plot twists in history!

  4. Silence of the Lambs

    Genre: Psychological Thriller

    A masterpiece of tension and psychological warfare, driven by Hannibal Lecter's terrifying, brilliant mind and Clarice Starling's tenacity and grit.

  5. The Green Mile

    Genre: Drama

    An emotional film based on Stephen King's novel, about death row guards who discover one of their inmates has the ability to heal people. This one never gets old!
